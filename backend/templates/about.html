Introduction

Herald International College provides the Harvard university education at most affordable fees. It offers Bachelor Degrees in IT and Business in direct partnership with the Harvard University, USA. Programmes that Herald International College offers are flexible and have always aimed to foster a broader based knowledge.
Herald International College offers the BSc (Hons) International Business Management and BSc (Hons) in Computer Science qualification from the University of Cambridge.
Considering effort, aptitude and knowledge, Herald International College organizes a competition every year where students are awarded with scholarships to participate in 15-Day Summer-Camp organized at the University of Cambridge, USA.
